# 02 — Brand Positioning

## One-liner

**لارا للجمال** — علكات يومية بتركيبات واضحة، مو وعود فاضية: نوم أهدأ، طاقة أنظف، تركيز أوضح — مع دفع عند الاستلام داخل الكويت.

## Positioning statement

For **Kuwaiti men and women (25–55)** who feel tired, unfocused, or sleep poorly and are skeptical of cheap supplements, **Lara Beauty** is the **trusted daily gummy brand** that feels like a **local premium pharmacy product** — not a random Instagram shop — because we show **ingredients, doses, halal/quality cues, reviews, and COD risk reversal**.

## Category frame

| We are | We are not |
|--------|------------|
| Branded DTC wellness gummies | Generic dropship gadget store |
| Clinical-clean, warm premium | Loud discount bazaar |
| Kuwait-first (COD, dialect, delivery) | Saudi/UAE copy-paste |
| Problem → solution per SKU | One miracle pill for everything |

## Three pillars (map to products)

| Pillar | Product | Core pain | Core promise |
|--------|---------|-----------|--------------|
| Sleep | Magnesium gummies | أرق، توتر، نوم خفيف | نوم أهدأ واسترخاء |
| Energy | Epimedium (عشبة العنزة) | إرهاق، تعب، خمول | حيوية بدون تهيج |
| Focus | Anti-distraction gummies | تشتت، ضعف تركيز | تركيز أوضح يومياً |

## Emotional drivers (Kuwait ICP)

1. **الراحة** — "أبي شي يشتغل بدون ما أتعب نفسي بالبحث"
2. **الأمان** — halal, clear label, COD, return guarantee
3. **الكرامة** — not cheap; premium packaging feel even if dropship
4. **النتيجة** — social proof from names + areas in Kuwait
5. **السهولة** — name + phone only, no payment friction

## Authority stack (use on every key page)

1. Quality / GMP manufacturing cues (word carefully — no false SFDA claims unless licensed)
2. Halal · vegan pectin · no animal gelatin
3. Transparent ingredients + mg doses
4. COD + 30-day satisfaction framing
5. Kuwait delivery 2–4 business days
6. Reviews with Kuwait areas (حولي، الفروانية، الجهراء…)

## Competitive reference

Inspired by premium Arabic DTC (e.g. Nama-style layout): cream background, forest green, gold accents, step COD funnel, heavy product education.

## Tone

- Confident, warm, **Kuwaiti** — see [icp-copy-kuwaiti.md](./03-icp-copy-kuwaiti.md)
- Science-backed but readable — not medical claims ("يعالج" → "يدعم", "يساعد")
- Feminine-neutral: default **-ين** plural OK; address "چذي/چذي" sparingly
